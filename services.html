Our Scope

Expertise
Pathways

A structured framework designed to guide candidates through examinations, admissions, and professional pivots.

01

Study Abroad Consulting

We guide students and families through the selection of international academic pathways. By evaluating transcripts, budget specifications, and career targets, we match candidates to suitable universities in global destinations including the UK, US, Canada, and Europe.

Advisory Scope:

  • • Country and university matching metrics
  • • Admission application review & submission timeline planning
  • • Visa documentation advisory & interview preparation
  • • Relocation guidelines & pre-departure briefings
02

Standardized Test Preparation

Targeting competitive global admissions requires verified performance baselines. We provide structured training classrooms and simulated examination environments to prepare candidates for major international standardized tests.

Preparatory Modules:

  • • SAT classes for global undergraduate pathways
  • • IELTS & TOEFL language competency prep
  • • GMAT & GRE modules for graduate admissions
  • • Diagnostic score reports & full computer-based mock exams
03

Local Academic Support

Delivered through our foundational academic brand, DTW Tutorials. We operate a massive online STEM video learning community focusing on WAEC, NECO, and JAMB UTME. We bridge the local curriculum to build solid preparation foundations.

Academic Resources:

  • • Free step-by-step Math and Physics video tutorials on YouTube
  • • Interactive JAMB CBT Practice Software for mock testing
  • • Syllabus-aligned WAEC & NECO prep guides via DTW EduStore
  • • Diagnostic reviews for national secondary school exams
04

University Admission Consulting

We collaborate with candidates to compile, audit, and optimize their university application profiles. From course selection strategy to credit matching, we ensure student folders conform to specific institutional benchmarks.

Advisory Scope:

  • • Statement of Purpose (SOP) critique and editing workshops
  • • CV rebuilds tailored for university admission review
  • • Transcript translation & credential audits (WES, ECE)
  • • Transfer opportunities & alternative path mappings
05

Career & Academic Guidance

Helping students and graduates identify academic strengths, evaluate career options, and design long-term growth plans. We align school subjects and university credentials with emerging opportunities.

Key Elements:

  • • Academic capability diagnostic assessments
  • • Certification alignment and pathway mapping
  • • LinkedIn positioning for transition candidates
  • • Mock interviews for academic and career placements
06

Educational Events

DTW Consult hosts periodic events to connect candidates with opportunities. We bring together university representatives, test preparation instructors, and career advisors under one roof.

Event Formats:

  • • In-person university admission clinics
  • • Diagnostic SAT & IELTS mock test events
  • • Strategic postgraduate career seminars
  • • Parent information briefings on relocation guidelines
07
Expanding Division

Technology Training

Preparing candidates for modern workspaces through structured digital training program classes. We focus on building portfolios of practical tech skills to support career transitions and global hiring targets.

Skill Paths:

  • • Data Analytics (SQL & Python fundamentals)
  • • Product Design classes (UI/UX wireframing & prototyping)
  • • Digital literacy courses & work productivity tools
  • • Professional certification preparation pathways
