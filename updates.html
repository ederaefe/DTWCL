DTW Summer Bootcamp 2026 Is Now Online

Registration is officially open for the DTW Summer Bootcamp 2026;
a free online learning experience designed to help children and teenagers (ages 8–17) develop practical digital skills for the future.

Over five interactive days, participants will receive hands-on training from experienced instructors in some of today's most valuable tech disciplines.

Featured Courses

- Artificial Intelligence (AI) Creation
- Graphic Design
- Video Editing
- Leadership & Personal Development
- And more

Program Details

- Date: August 10–14, 2026
- Time: 9:00 AM – 1:00 PM
- Location: Online
- Cost: Free (Participants should have access to a laptop or smartphone.)

Limited spaces are available. Register early to secure your spot.
