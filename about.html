The Rebrand & Focus

DTW Consult was founded on a simple premise: educational decisions dictate the trajectory of a student's life. What began as a dedicated STEM channel creating physics and math videos to support students through WAEC and JAMB evolved into DTW Tutorials.

As the scope of our students' needs grew, we recognized a critical gap between passing secondary exams and successfully transitioning into global universities. A high score is merely a credential; navigating admission matrices, SOP structures, visa guidelines, and credit evaluation audits is a separate strategic undertaking.

To address this, we rebranded from a localized coaching group to a comprehensive educational consulting firm. Today, DTW Consult operates as an academic advisory partner, building preparation schedules and coordinating admissions portfolios under a unified standard.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To remove uncertainty from educational decisions by providing students and parents with expert guidance, structured preparation, and a transparent path toward academic milestones.

Our Vision

To serve as the primary educational advisory bridge across West Africa, guiding students successfully from secondary classrooms to global university acceptance and career placements.
